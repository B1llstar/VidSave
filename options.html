VidSave

Videos save into this subfolder under your browser's Downloads folder.

Downloads /
To change where your browser's Downloads folder itself points (e.g. to an external drive or a different location), open brave://settings/downloads — VidSave will follow whatever that's set to.
Tip: On any page with a video, hover over it and click the ⬇ save button that appears in the corner. Files are named after the page title automatically and saved with zero dialogs.
